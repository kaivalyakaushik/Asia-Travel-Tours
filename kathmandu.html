About Kathmandu

Kathmandu is a city of ancient temples, prayer flags, and stunning mountain views. Nestled in a valley surrounded by the Himalayas, it offers rich cultural heritage, spiritual experiences, and adventure opportunities.

Specialties

  • Himalayan mountain views
  • Ancient Buddhist and Hindu temples
  • Spiritual and meditation centers
  • Trekking base for Everest
  • Traditional arts and crafts
  • Authentic Nepali cuisine
